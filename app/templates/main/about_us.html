Our Objectives

Promote a Reading Culture

Our raffles feature a diverse selection of books across genres, ensuring that there's something for every reader. From fiction to non-fiction, classics to contemporary releases, our raffles offer a curated literary experience.

Encourage Kenyan Authors

{{ app_name }} is dedicated to supporting and promoting the incredible talent of Kenyan authors. Through our platform, we aim to provide them with a broader audience and recognition for their literary contributions.

Raise Funds for Charity

Beyond the realms of books, {{ app_name }} is driven by a strong sense of social responsibility. We aim to contribute to various charitable causes by channeling the proceeds from our book raffle system to initiatives that make a positive impact on communities.

Our Beneficiaries

Nduundune Primary School Feeding Program

Supporting the school's feeding program, we aim to address the critical needs outlined by the headmaster. The success of this initiative will serve as a model for other areas, multiplying the impact on a national scale.

Alliance High School Endowment Fund Trust

Contributing to scholarships for needy students admitted to Alliance High School, {{ app_name }} seeks to empower young minds through education.

Cancer Advocacy

As cancer warriors ourselves, we are deeply involved in cancer activism and patient support. Collaborating with cancer advocacy groups, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of those affected by this challenging journey.
