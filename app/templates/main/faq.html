Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to our FAQ page. We've compiled answers to common questions about our platform, how it works, and what you can expect when participating in book raffles. If you have a question that isn't covered here, feel free to Contact Us for further assistance.

General Questions

What is the {{ app_name }}'s OBRS?

It is a web-based platform designed to promote a reading culture, support Kenyan authors, and raise funds for charitable causes through book raffles.

How does it work?

It allows users to purchase raffle tickets for a chance to win books. Participants can browse a selection of books, purchase tickets, and, when the required participant count is reached, a winner is randomly selected.

Is the OBRS legal and regulated?

Yes, the OBRS adheres to all necessary regulations set forth by the Betting Control and Licensing Board, ensuring a legal and ethical operation.

Account and Registration

How do I register on the OBRS?

To register, click on the "Sign Up" button and provide your name, email address, and create a password.

Is registration free?

Yes, registration on the OBRS is free.

Can I change my account information?

You can update your account information by accessing your profile settings after logging in.

Book Raffles

How can I participate in a book raffle?

To participate, browse the available books, select a book, and purchase raffle tickets. Once the required participant count is reached, a random draw is conducted.

How much do raffle tickets cost?

The price of raffle tickets may vary for each book. The ticket price is displayed on the book's raffle page.

How are winners selected?

Winners are selected through a random draw when the required number of participants is met.

Charitable Contributions

How are charitable contributions made?

A percentage of the proceeds from each raffle goes towards supporting charitable initiatives, as indicated on the raffle page.

Can I choose which charity receives the contribution from my raffle ticket purchase?

Charitable contributions are directed to the specified charitable causes as outlined on each raffle page.
